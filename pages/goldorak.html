UFO Robot Grendizer : The feast of the Wolves is an action-aventure game developped by Endroad and edited by Microïds.

Official Website

Built using Unity Engine, and developped for PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, PS5, Switch, Xbox Series X|S and Xbox One, UFO Robot Grendizer : The feast of the Wolves brings to life the legendary robot, in an immersive semi-open world.

Play as Actarus and meet many essential characters during dialogue sequences in the iconic environments of the series, faithfully reproduced in respect of the designs of the original series.
